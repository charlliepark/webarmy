    The Salvation Army family is made up of church members, volunteers, employees, supporters and people involved in our community and social work. We put our Christian faith into action and offer practical support and services to all who need them, regardless of ethnicity, religion, gender or sexual orientation. Find out more about our work. The United Kingdom and Republic of Ireland is part of the wider international Salvation Army, which changes lives in 131 countries around the world. Your support allows us to respond to the people who need us most. Find out ways to get involved below.
